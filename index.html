DexTube

DexTube is a pioneering platform in the decentralized science (DeSci) ecosystem. Our mission is to revolutionize how scientific research is funded by leveraging NFT technology to create a transparent, equitable, and community-driven fundraising mechanism. DexTube empowers researchers, institutions, and contributors to co-own and collaborate on groundbreaking scientific advancements through decentralized ownership of research outcomes.

NFTs That Power Scientific Research

At DexTube, we believe in a smarter way to fund science. Researchers mint their projects as unique NFTs, each representing milestones, data, or intellectual property. Supporters can purchase, stake, or trade these NFTs, fueling progress while building a direct link between funding and impact.

How DexTube Works

01

Submit Your Project

Ready to take your research to the next level? Submit your project proposal—outlining your goals, required funding, and milestones—and let the community weigh in.

02

Mint Your NFTs

Once your project is approved, you'll mint NFTs that represent different research milestones, intellectual property, or outcomes. Each NFT holds value—and it's yours to share with the world.

03

Funding Made Simple

Supporters can now easily fund your research by purchasing or staking your NFTs. The more they invest, the more they become part of your project's success.

04

Progress Updates in Real-Time

You'll keep your backers updated with regular progress reports and deliverables. Everyone stays informed, and no one is left behind.

05

Unlock Real-World Impact

When your project reaches its goal, supporters share in the intellectual property rights, royalties, or early access to your innovation. It's a win-win.
